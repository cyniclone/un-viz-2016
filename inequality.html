Reduced Inequalities

TARGET: By 2030, progressively achieve and sustain income growth of the bottom 40 per cent of the population at a rate higher than the national average

HOW TO READ THIS CHART: The position on the horizontal scale represents the rate of growth of the income of either the entire population ( circle) or the poorest 40% ( circle) of people on each country. The greater the length of the connecting line, the greater the disparity of income growth rate.
