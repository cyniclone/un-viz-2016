How to Read This Chart

  1. Each circle is a country
  2. The position of each country on the vertical axis is the number of children per 1,000 live births who die before the age of 5.

Target for 2030:

By 2030, end preventable deaths of newborns and children under 5 years of age, with all countries aiming to reduce neonatal mortality to no more than 12 per 1,000 live births and mortality of children under 5 to no more than 25 per 1,000 live births
